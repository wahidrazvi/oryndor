Our Heritage

Rooted in
Kashmir's Soul

Nestled among the Himalayas, our story begins in the sunlit valleys of Jammu & Kashmir — where the soil is mineral-rich, the air is crisp, and every chilly carries the memory of the mountains.

At Oryndor Tandoor, we believe that authentic spice begins with authentic soil. Our red chillies are handpicked at peak ripeness, sun-dried on traditional khats, and stone-ground using century-old techniques that preserve every volatile oil and flavour compound.

No artificial heat. No additives. Just the earth, the sun, and generations of knowledge.
